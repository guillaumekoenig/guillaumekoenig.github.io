Proof strategies

[2020-05-29 Fri]

Working on a proof involves rewriting the givens and the goal. It ends when all the steps of going from givens to the goal have been spelled out.

Prove (goal) : assume (given), prove (goal). This can be applied repeatedly for multiple conditionals. Another possibility is to prove the contrapositive . There is no hard and fast rule to which is better, try one and if it doesn't work, try the other.

Prove (goal) : it's usually easier to prove a positive statement. Try to transform it by distributing the negation. This is not always possible, ie if doesn't contain logical connectives. In general, if it's not obvious how to reach the goal, proof by contradiction might work : assume the negation of the goal, and reach a contradiction with one of the other givens.
