A few notes on chapter 1 of /Microbiology for dummies/.

- What's a microorganism living with host yielding mutual benefit ? A symbiont.
- What's a microorganism living with host causing disease ? A pathogen.
- What does micro refers to, in microorganism ? They're a few micrometers in size.
- What's a microbe ? Same thing as a microorganism.
- What does /karyote/ means in {eu,pro}karyote ? Kernel (coming from Greek).
- Eukaryote vs prokaryote ? With kernel and without kernel.
- Common point of microorganisms ? All hold genetic material.
- Where is the genetic material stored in eukaryotes ? in the kernel ("nucleus")
- Where is the genetic material stored in prokaryotes ? in a kernel-like shape ("nucleoid") floating in the cell
- Subdivisions of prokaryotes ? Bacteria and Archaea. They're unicellular.
- Two particularities of viruses ? They're made of genetic material, but they're not alive on their own.
