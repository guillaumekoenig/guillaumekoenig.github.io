#+TITLE: Ditching Pocket
#+DATE: [2019-10-10 Thu]

It sounded like a good idea. Synchronize with your phone. Save a link now and read later.

I've been doing it for a few years. An export of all the links shows there are 218 of them. So what is the problem ?

(1) the list of pending articles to read gives a sense of chore. (2) looking at the export makes me realize I've barely gone back to re-read any article (and you know that's how you remember anything). (3) passive consumption leaves me with the impression of not having retained much.

I am stopping now.
